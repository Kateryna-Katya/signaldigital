Pawpop Match

About the Game

Pawpop Match combines familiar match-3 mechanics with charming characters that react to player progress. Each level feels connected to helping and interacting with pets.
What really sets this apart is how the pets react to what you’re doing — it just makes you want
to keep playing.

Features

  • Character-driven progression

  • Bright and playful visuals

  • Match-3 gameplay with pet companions

  • Smooth difficulty curve

  • Short and accessible sessions

  • Cozy carnival adventure atmosphere

Game Feel

Light, joyful, rewarding

We wanted players to feel happy and relaxed when they play. Bright colors, responsive animations, and character reactions create a sense of constant reward.
It’s the kind of game you can chill with but still get totally absorbed in.

Characters

Felix the Fox

The cheerful ringmaster of the traveling circus. Always confident, energetic, and ready to lead the adventure.

Ricky the Raccoon

A playful and curious companion who loves puzzles, surprises, and collecting shiny rewards.

Pip the Duckling

The smartest member of the group. Tiny, funny, and always observing everything through oversized glasses.
